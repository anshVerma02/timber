Our Products

Teak Wood

Known for its durability and natural resistance to termites and water, Teak wood is the preferred choice for furniture, doors, and premium interiors.

Meranti Wood

Meranti is a versatile hardwood, ideal for furniture, paneling, and joinery. Known for its smooth finish and easy workability, we supply premium Meranti wood for multiple uses.

Pine Wood

Lightweight, affordable, and versatile, Pine wood is perfect for furniture, paneling, and decorative items. We deliver superior quality Pine for multiple purposes.

Sal Wood

Sal wood is renowned for its exceptional strength, making it ideal for structural work, beams, and heavy furniture. We offer premium Sal wood for lasting durability.

Door Frames

Window Frames

Wooden Planks
