Our Manufacturing Process

We follow a structured 11-step process to ensure unmatched precision, quality, and durability in every wood product we deliver.

1

LOG SAWING

Cutting raw logs into workable sections for further processing

2

SIZE CUTTING

Trimming wood into required dimensions as per specifications.

3

SORTING

Organizing cut pieces based on size, quality, and intended use.

4

SEASONING

Drying wood to reduce moisture content and prevent warping.

5

CHEMICAL TREATMENT

Protecting wood from termites and other insects.

6

RIP SAWING

Cutting wood along the grain for precision sizing.

7

PLANING

Smoothing and leveling the wood surface for uniform thickness.

8

FINGER CUTTING

Creating interlocking cuts to join wood pieces securely.

9

JOINTING

Aligning and attaching finger-cut wood pieces together.

10

SANDING

Finishing the surface by removing imperfections for a smooth touch.

11

FINAL PRODUCT

A Wide Range of Ready-to-Ship Timber Products, Crafted for Your Needs.
